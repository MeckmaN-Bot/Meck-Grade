Scan tips for best results
  • Scan at 600 DPI (300 DPI minimum)
  • Place card face-down on scanner glass
  • Put a dark paper sheet on top of the card — helps boundary detection
  • Use TIFF or PNG (lossless) for maximum accuracy
  • Do not resize or apply filters to the image before uploading
  • Scan front and back separately
